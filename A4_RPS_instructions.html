The Element Game: Instructions

Welcome to The Element Game! The element version of Rock Paper Scissors!

The Element Game is a very simple game that has the same objecive as rock paper scissors: the player chooses an element from your given options. Each element can kill two other elements and get killed by two other elements.

Your choices:

  • Water
  • Fire
  • Electricity
  • Air
  • Earth

Two Modes:

  • 1 Player

    • The one player mode is designed for players to play against an AI generator. The odds are even. Since there are 5 choices the odds of each element being chosen is 20%. Your choice and the generated element will be displated each round. You can choose the score you would like to play to prior to beginning the game.

  • 2 Player

    • The two player mode is designed for those who have a friend with them and prefer to play against them. The game will offer each player a chance to choose an element. For the game to be fair we recommend each player to look away while the choice is being made by the other player. Both of the players choices will be displayed after each round. You can choose the score you would like to play to prior to beginning the game.

The Power of The Elements

Element Can Eliminate Eliminated By
Water Fire, Earth Air, Electricity
Fire Earth, Electricity Water, Air
Electricity Water, Air Fire, Earth
Air Fire, Water Earth, Electricity
Earth Air, Electricity Fire, Water

Winning and Loosing

You gain points based on the choices of elements. If your oponent choose an element that eliminates you, you loose. If your you choose an element that eliminates your oponenet, you win.

You gain a score everytime you win and the first to reach the score you chose to go up to is the winner of all rounds.

Ties / Draw

A draw occurs when both opponents choose the same elements.

Enjoy The Game!!
